Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Membership Fee Refund Policy

At Professionals Solidarity Forum (PSF), we value your membership and want to ensure your satisfaction with our services. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding refunds for membership fees.

Annual Membership Fee

Our refund policy for annual membership fees is as follows:

  • Within 7 days of payment: Full refund (less payment processing fees)
  • 8-30 days after payment: 50% refund
  • After 30 days: No refund

Lifetime Membership Fee

Our refund policy for lifetime membership fees is as follows:

  • Within 14 days of payment: Full refund (less payment processing fees)
  • 15-60 days after payment: 50% refund
  • After 60 days: No refund

Membership Cancellation

You may cancel your membership at any time by:

  • Logging into your account and selecting the cancellation option
  • Contacting our support team at contact@psf-india.org
  • Calling our customer service at +91-998-877-3493

Please note that cancellation of membership does not automatically entitle you to a refund. Refund eligibility is determined by the timeframes outlined above.

Event Registration Refunds

For PSF events, workshops, and conferences, the following refund policy applies:

  • Cancellation 30+ days before the event: Full refund (less processing fees)
  • Cancellation 15-29 days before the event: 50% refund
  • Cancellation 0-14 days before the event: No refund

In case of event cancellation by PSF, attendees will be offered either a full refund or credit toward a future event.

Exceptional Circumstances

We understand that exceptional circumstances may arise. In cases of serious illness, bereavement, or other exceptional circumstances, we may consider refund requests outside of the standard policy on a case-by-case basis. Supporting documentation may be required.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team with the following information:

  • Your full name and membership ID
  • Date of payment
  • Reason for refund request
  • Payment method used

Refund requests can be submitted via:

  • Email: contact@psf-india.org
  • Phone: +91-998-877-3493
  • In writing to our registered address

Refund Processing

Once approved, refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days. The refund will be issued using the original payment method unless otherwise specified. Please note that it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your payment provider's policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our refund and cancellation policy from time to time. Any changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
