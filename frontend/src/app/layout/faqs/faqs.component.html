Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: May 2025

General Questions

What is Professionals Solidarity Forum (PSF)?

PSF is a professional organization dedicated to supporting and connecting professionals across various industries. We provide networking opportunities, professional development resources, and advocacy for our members.

 How do I become a member?

You can become a member by registering on our website and completing the membership application process. Membership requires payment of applicable fees as outlined in our Terms & Conditions.

Membership Questions

What are the membership fees?

We offer monthly and custom options. For specific pricing details, please visit our membership page or contact us directly.

 Can I get a refund for my membership?

Refund policies vary depending on membership type and timing of cancellation. Please refer to our detailed Refund & Cancellation Policy for specific information.

 How do I cancel my membership?

You can cancel your membership by logging into your account, contacting our support team at contact@psf-india.org, or calling +91-998-877-3493. Note that cancellation does not automatically entitle you to a refund.

Privacy & Data

What information does PSF collect?

We collect various types of personal data as outlined in our Privacy Policy, including identity data, contact information, professional details, and technical usage data.

 How is my data protected?

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.

 Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes, you have the right to request erasure of your personal data under certain circumstances as described in our Privacy Policy.
