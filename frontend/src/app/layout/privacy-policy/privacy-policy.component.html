Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Introduction

At Professionals Solidarity Forum (PSF), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Information We Collect

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data: first name, last name, username or similar identifier, title.
  • Contact Data: email address, telephone numbers, and postal address.
  • Professional Data: occupation, employer, professional qualifications and experience.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, time zone, OS, and device info.
  • Usage Data: how you use our website and services.
  • Financial Data: payment details for membership fees and donations.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data in the following circumstances:

Membership Management

Register and manage your membership and its benefits.

Communication

Maintain communication and handle requests or feedback.

Improvement

Analyze website usage to enhance user experience.

Security

Ensure organizational and website protection.

Data Security

We implement robust measures to secure your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Only authorized personnel have access.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for legal or operational purposes.

Your Legal Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Request transfer of data
  • Withdraw consent anytime
